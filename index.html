You will be given a random Match Number at the start of the game.

On the right, you will find four crystals. By clicking on the crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

The value of each crystal is hidden. You have to click to find out, and remember it once you do.

Each time the game starts, the value of the crystals will change.
